Avocado Salad

A fresh, creamy salad featuring sliced avocado and thinly sliced red onions, drizzled with a tangy mayo-cream lemon dressing, and garnished with chopped green coriander. Perfect as a side for a light meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, sliced into thin rings
  • 4 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 4 tbsp fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Green chutney (as per taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Chopped green coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Slice the avocado and mix with lemon juice, then layer in a serving dish.
  2. Thinly slice the red onion into rings and spread evenly over the avocado.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, fresh cream, lemon juice, green chutney, and salt to form the dressing.
  4. Pour the dressing over the avocado and onion mixture.
  5. Gently mix everything together and top with chopped coriander. Serve immediately.
